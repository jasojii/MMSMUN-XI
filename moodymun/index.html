About Model UN

Welcome to MoodyMUN! Model United Nations is a tremendous activity that creates a great culture filled with fun. Through Model UN, students learn to hone their debating skills, create new friends, and win awards. Model UN is a mock version of the real United Nations. In the process, delegates learn about the workings of the real United Nations. In conclusion, the experience of Model UN is a opportunity for students to broaden their horizons and learn more about the governing bodies of the world. We hope to see you here on conference day!
